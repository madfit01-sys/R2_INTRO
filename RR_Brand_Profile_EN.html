THE STORY BEHIND RHYTHM & REVOLUTION
Credibility earned on the professional field.
Now shared with the entire world.
Rhythm & Revolution began where most fitness brands never go — inside professional sports. For 8 years, Master Lee developed and refined Sport Rhythm Training (SRT) exclusively for Korea's elite athletes across 5 professional sports disciplines. The credibility earned there became the foundation for everything that followed.

From that foundation, 400+ purposeful Rhythm Performance Training programs were researched and developed — each one designed not as routine exercise, but as a purposeful training experience with clear goals for the brain and body.

In October 2025, R&R made a bold decision: to share what had been built for champions with everyone — infants, children, teenagers, working adults, and seniors. The response was immediate and global. In just 6 months, 91 million views. And we have only shown a small part of what we've created.

2026 is our turning point. Thoroughly prepared. Globally positioned. Ready to bring more joyful, purposeful movement to people everywhere.
